ABOUT CATCH CHARITABLE TRUST

We measurably want to reduce the burden of poverty for hunderds of millions of people. Our charitable trust is set upin order to tackle the problems of poverty especially among young children. Catch Charitable Trust is committed towelfare of people from the most under privileged society without distinction of caste,creed or color.It's emphasis is on assisting the children and youth who are the citizens of tommorow and the disabled who need a support to help them join main stream society.

Our logo symbolises our vision for a future. A future in which each and every child can look forward to a new day,a day free of hunger,suffering,abuse and poverty. We want to reach out to children across the world to give them new opportunities,and help them rise out of poverty. Our mission is to educate and empower underprivileged children and suport them till they are gainfully employed. Our vission is to make a happy,healthy and creative child whose rights are proctected and honoured in a society that is built on respect for dignity,justice and equality for all. Our mission is to enable people to tke responsibility for the situation every indian and so motivate them to seek resolution through individual and collective action thereby enabling them to realise their full potential.
