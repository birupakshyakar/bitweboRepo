About Us

ROYAL Institute recognizes that modern educational and technical research is carried out in interstices amongst fuzzy domains and blurred boundaries. This entails encouraging a new culture where our students compete in today's market nad establish themselves in a well professional manner. Faculty and Students are given generous material support in the pursuit to realize this objective.


It has been the constant endeavour of ROYAL Institute to provide the best course material for the students desirous of taking up the exams. Our team of talented faculties have prepared the study material in a lucid, concise and easy way to understand.

VISION

The vision of ROYAL Institute is to be a leading global technological Institution that provides a transformative education to create leaders and innovators and generates new knowledge for society eradicating darkness of ignorance. Our aim is to create a new generation with our enormous facilities that can compete in all respect globally.

MISSION

The mission of ROYAL Institute is to impart quality education which will help in the all round development of the students. Our effort is to develop values like discipline, hardwork, team spirits, social responsibility, scientific tempers and critical thinking in our students. We want our students to be influential leaders and innovators in various spheres of life.

Mobile application for all

Frequently conducted differnet debate, quiz, art, music, dance, sports, rangoli and other competitions.

Examination

We conduct regular examinations such as Theory and Practical test. Assesment test, Chapter wise test and Class practice test are conducted in every week. The result of each exam is informed to parents by the Software management system.

Assignment

The trained teachers verify the assignment of each students everyday. The pecrentage of assignment done and the percentage of attendance are regularly informed to their guardians.
